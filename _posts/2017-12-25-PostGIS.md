## 导入数据

## python从postgis中读取数据

```python
import psycopg2
conn = psycopg2.connect(database="gisdb", user="postgres",
                        password="*******", host="127.0.0.1",
                        port="5432")
cur = conn.cursor()
cur.execite("SELECT * FROM public.chn_adm0")
rows = cur.fetchall()
descpition = cur.description
```
